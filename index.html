SHARKS

My favorite animals have always been sharks, because I see them as beautiful sea creatures who are important to our enviornment and super cool! There are so many types of sharks, and although they can be dangerous, they are also stunning animals that live very interesting lives! Many movies and TV shows paint sharks to be bloodthirsty preditors, but I believe they deserve a different image.

THE TIGER SHARK

  • Tiger sharks can grow up to 18 feet long
  • Tiger sharks can weigh up to 1,400+ lbs
  • They are known as the "dustbin of the sea"
  • Their unique stripes fade as they mature
  • Serrated teeth that can bite through turtle shells
  • Often hunted for their teeth and skin
